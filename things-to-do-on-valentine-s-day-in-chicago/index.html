Things To Do On Valentine s Day In Chicago

Valentine's Day is just around the corner, and if you're in Chicago, you're in luck! There are plenty of romantic things to do in the Windy City. Whether you're looking for a romantic dinner, a stroll through the city, or a unique experience, there's something for everyone. Here are some of the best things to do on Valentine's Day in Chicago.

1. Take a Romantic Stroll Through Millennium Park

Millennium Park is a beautiful place to take a romantic stroll with your loved one. The park is home to the famous Cloud Gate sculpture, also known as "The Bean," which is a great spot for taking a photo together. You can also enjoy the park's beautiful gardens, fountains, and artwork.

2. Enjoy a Romantic Dinner at The Signature Room at the 95th

The Signature Room at the 95th is a great spot for a romantic dinner with breathtaking views of the city. The restaurant is located on the 95th floor of the John Hancock Center and offers a menu of American cuisine. Make sure to make a reservation in advance to secure a table with a view.

3. Go Ice Skating at Maggie Daley Park

If you're looking for a fun and romantic activity, head to Maggie Daley Park for ice skating. The park has a beautiful ice skating rink with stunning views of the city skyline. You can rent skates at the rink and enjoy a fun-filled afternoon or evening with your loved one.

4. Take a River Cruise

Chicago's river cruises are a great way to see the city's famous architecture while enjoying a romantic ride on the river. You can choose from a variety of cruises, including sunset and dinner cruises. Make sure to book your tickets in advance, as they tend to sell out quickly.

5. Visit the Art Institute of Chicago

The Art Institute of Chicago is a great spot for art lovers and a perfect place to spend a romantic day. The museum houses an impressive collection of art, including works by famous artists such as Monet, Van Gogh, and Picasso. You can also enjoy the museum's beautiful gardens and architecture.

6. Enjoy a Romantic Picnic in Grant Park

Grant Park is a beautiful spot for a romantic picnic with your loved one. The park offers stunning views of Lake Michigan and the Chicago skyline. You can pack a picnic lunch and enjoy the scenery while spending quality time with your partner.

7. Catch a Show at the Chicago Theatre

The Chicago Theatre is a historic landmark and a great spot for a romantic night out. The theatre hosts a variety of shows, including concerts, comedy acts, and plays. Make sure to check the schedule and book your tickets in advance.

8. Visit the Lincoln Park Zoo

The Lincoln Park Zoo is a great spot for animal lovers and a perfect place for a romantic day out. The zoo is home to a wide variety of animals, including lions, tigers, and bears. You can also enjoy the zoo's beautiful gardens and architecture.

9. Take a Romantic Helicopter Tour

If you're looking for a unique and romantic experience, take a helicopter tour of the city. You can enjoy stunning views of the Chicago skyline and Lake Michigan while spending quality time with your loved one.

10. Relax at a Spa

If you're looking for a relaxing and romantic activity, head to a spa for a couples massage or other treatments. Chicago has many spas to choose from, including the luxurious Peninsula Spa and the Four Seasons Spa.

Conclusion

There are plenty of romantic things to do on Valentine's Day in Chicago. Whether you're looking for a romantic dinner, a stroll through the city, or a unique experience, there's something for everyone. Make sure to plan ahead and book your reservations in advance to ensure a romantic and unforgettable Valentine's Day with your loved one.
