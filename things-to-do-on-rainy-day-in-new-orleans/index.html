Things to Do on a Rainy Day in New Orleans - Tips and Solutions

Are you in New Orleans and experiencing a rainy day? Don't let the weather stop you from enjoying this vibrant city. There are plenty of fun things to do on a rainy day in New Orleans. Here are some tips and solutions to help you make the most of your time.

Table of Contents

  • Visit Museums
  • Go Shopping
  • Listen to Live Music
  • Watch a Movie
  • Indulge in Food and Drink

Visit Museums

New Orleans is home to several museums that are perfect for a rainy day. The National World War II Museum is a must-see attraction for history buffs. The New Orleans Museum of Art features a vast collection of contemporary and traditional art. The Ogden Museum of Southern Art showcases the works of Southern artists. You can spend hours exploring these museums and learning something new.

Tip:

Check the museum's website for special exhibits and events. Some museums offer discounted admission on rainy days.

Go Shopping

Shopping is a great way to spend a rainy day in New Orleans. The French Quarter has many unique shops and boutiques. You can find everything from antiques to locally made souvenirs. The Outlet Collection at Riverwalk is also a popular shopping destination. It features over 75 stores, including major brands like Coach, Levi's, and Nike.

Tip:

Bring an umbrella or raincoat to stay dry between stores. Also, many shops in the French Quarter offer free samples of local food and drink.

Listen to Live Music

New Orleans is known as the birthplace of jazz music. You can listen to live music in many venues throughout the city, even on a rainy day. The Spotted Cat Music Club, located in the French Quarter, is a popular spot for jazz and blues. The Maison on Frenchmen Street also features live music every night.

Tip:

Check the venue's website for the schedule of performers. Some venues offer happy hour specials during the early evening hours.

Watch a Movie

If you're in the mood for a movie, head to one of New Orleans' historic theaters. The Prytania Theatre has been showing movies since 1915 and features both classic and current films. The Orpheum Theater, built in 1918, hosts special screenings and events.

Tip:

Bring some snacks and enjoy the movie in the comfort of a historic theater.

Indulge in Food and Drink

New Orleans is known for its cuisine and cocktails. Use a rainy day as an excuse to try some new restaurants and bars. The city has many options for every taste and budget. From po'boys to gumbo to beignets, there's something for everyone.

Tip:

Check out happy hour specials and restaurant deals on rainy days. Some places offer discounts to attract customers.

Frequently Asked Questions

What should I wear on a rainy day in New Orleans?

Bring an umbrella or raincoat to stay dry. Also, wear comfortable shoes that can handle wet sidewalks and streets.

Are there any indoor activities in New Orleans?

Yes, there are many indoor activities in New Orleans, including museums, shopping, live music, and movies.

What are some popular restaurants in New Orleans?

New Orleans has many famous restaurants, including Commander's Palace, Brennan's, and Antoine's. However, there are also many hidden gems that offer delicious food at a lower price point.

Pros and Cons

Pros:

  • Plenty of indoor activities to enjoy on a rainy day
  • Opportunities to try new food and drinks
  • Chance to explore museums and learn something new

Cons:

  • Weather may limit outdoor activities
  • Crowds at popular indoor destinations
  • Potential for traffic delays due to weather

Overall, there are many things to do on a rainy day in New Orleans. Whether you prefer museums, shopping, live music, or food, there's something for everyone. Don't let a little rain dampen your mood. Get out and explore this vibrant city.
