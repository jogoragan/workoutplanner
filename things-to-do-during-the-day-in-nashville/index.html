If you're looking for things to do during the day in Nashville, you've come to the right place! Nashville is a bustling city with plenty of activities to keep you busy all day long. Whether you're a history buff, a nature lover, or a foodie, there's something for everyone in Music City.

History

Nashville is steeped in history, and there are plenty of ways to explore it during the day. Here are a few ideas:

The Hermitage

The Hermitage is the former home of President Andrew Jackson and is now a museum and historic site. You can take a guided tour of the mansion and grounds or explore on your own.

The Johnny Cash Museum

The Johnny Cash Museum is a must-see for any music or history lover. The museum features exhibits on Cash's life and career, as well as artifacts and memorabilia.

Nature

Nashville is home to some beautiful parks and outdoor spaces that are perfect for enjoying during the day. Here are a few options:

Cumberland Park

Cumberland Park is located along the Cumberland River and features a playground, splash pad, and plenty of green space for picnics and games.

Cheekwood Estate & Gardens

Cheekwood Estate & Gardens is a 55-acre estate that features beautiful gardens, art exhibits, and a historic mansion. You can easily spend a whole day exploring this stunning property.

Food

Nashville is known for its food scene, and there are plenty of delicious options to try during the day. Here are a few suggestions:

The Loveless Cafe

The Loveless Cafe is a Nashville institution known for its Southern comfort food. Stop by for breakfast or lunch and enjoy some of the best biscuits in town.

The Pharmacy Burger

The Pharmacy Burger is a popular spot for burgers and beer. The outdoor beer garden is the perfect place to spend a sunny afternoon.

Music

No trip to Nashville is complete without some live music. Here are a few venues to check out during the day:

The Bluebird Cafe

The Bluebird Cafe is a small venue that's famous for its intimate performances. Many well-known musicians got their start here.

The Station Inn

The Station Inn is a legendary bluegrass venue that's been around since the '70s. Stop by during the day for some great music and a cold beer.

Shopping

Nashville has plenty of shopping options, from boutique clothing stores to record shops. Here are a few places to check out:

The Gulch

The Gulch is a trendy neighborhood with plenty of shops and restaurants. Stop by for some unique shopping options.

Grimey's New & Preloved Music

Grimey's is a beloved record shop that's been around since 1999. Stop by during the day to browse their extensive collection of new and used vinyl.

As you can see, there's no shortage of things to do during the day in Nashville. Whether you're interested in history, nature, food, music, or shopping, there's something for everyone in Music City.
