What To Do On Your Last Day In Vegas

Welcome to our guide on what to do on your last day in Vegas. As your vacation comes to a close, it's important to make the most of your final day in this iconic city. We've put together some tips and recommendations to help you end your trip on a high note. Let's dive in!

  2. Afternoon Activities

Morning Activities

Start your last day in Vegas with a delicious breakfast. Some popular breakfast spots include Eggslut at the Cosmopolitan, Hash House A Go Go at the LINQ, and Sadelle's at Bellagio. After breakfast, take a stroll down the Las Vegas Strip and enjoy the sights and sounds of the city.

If you're feeling adventurous, take a hot air balloon ride over the desert. This is a great way to see the city from a different perspective and create lasting memories. You can also take a helicopter tour of the Grand Canyon or Hoover Dam.

Afternoon Activities

Spend the afternoon exploring some of Vegas' iconic attractions. Visit the High Roller Observation Wheel for stunning views of the city, or check out the Stratosphere Tower for a thrilling ride on the SkyJump. You can also visit the Mob Museum or the National Atomic Testing Museum for a dose of history and culture.

If you're looking for some retail therapy, head to the Forum Shops at Caesars Palace, the Grand Canal Shoppes at the Venetian, or the Miracle Mile Shops at Planet Hollywood.

Evening Activities

End your trip with a bang by experiencing one of Vegas' world-renowned shows. Some popular options include Cirque du Soleil, Blue Man Group, and David Copperfield. You can also catch a concert or comedy show.

After the show, indulge in a delicious meal at one of Vegas' many restaurants. Some popular options include Joël Robuchon at MGM Grand, Twist by Pierre Gagnaire at the Mandarin Oriental, and é by José Andrés at the Cosmopolitan.

Tips for Your Last Day in Vegas

  • Make a list of your must-see attractions and prioritize them for your last day.
  • Consider booking activities and shows in advance to avoid disappointment.
  • Take advantage of hotel amenities such as pools, spas, and fitness centers.
  • Don't forget to pack and check out of your hotel before starting your activities for the day.

FAQ

What is the best way to get around Vegas?

The easiest way to get around Vegas is by foot or by using rideshare services such as Uber or Lyft. Taxis are also available, but can be more expensive.

What is the best time of year to visit Vegas?

The best time to visit Vegas is in the fall (September to November) or spring (March to May) when temperatures are mild and crowds are smaller.

What is the legal gambling age in Vegas?

The legal gambling age in Vegas is 21 years old.

We hope our guide on what to do on your last day in Vegas has been helpful. Remember to take your time and enjoy every moment of your trip. Safe travels!
