Are you a morning person? If you are, then you know that working out in the morning is one of the best ways to start your day. But let's face it, sometimes it's hard to get out of bed and get motivated. That's why we've compiled a list of funny early morning workout quotes that will make you laugh and get you pumped up for your morning workout.

Quote 1: "I always say, 'I'm not a morning person, but I'm a morning workout person.'" - Unknown

Starting your day with a workout can be tough, but once you get into the routine, it becomes easier. This quote reminds us that even if we're not naturally morning people, we can still make working out in the morning a habit.


Quote 2: "I don't have a six-pack, but I have a keg. That's kinda the same thing, right?" - Unknown

This funny quote is perfect for those who aren't quite at their fitness goals yet. It's a reminder that it's okay to have a little fun along the way.


Quote 3: "I don't need an alarm clock, my workout is my wake-up call." - Unknown

For those who struggle with waking up in the morning, this quote is a great motivator. It reminds us that our workout can be the perfect way to jumpstart our day.


Quote 4: "I work out because I know I would've been the first to die in the Hunger Games." - Unknown

This quote is perfect for any fans of the Hunger Games series. It's a fun way to remind ourselves that working out can help us be ready for anything.


Quote 5: "I may not be there yet, but I'm closer than I was yesterday." - Unknown

For those who are working towards their fitness goals, this quote is a great reminder that progress takes time. It's important to celebrate small victories along the way.


Quote 6: "I'm not a morning person or a night owl, I'm a permanently exhausted pigeon." - Unknown

This funny quote is perfect for those who struggle with getting up in the morning. It's a reminder that we're all a little tired sometimes, but we can still make time for our workouts.


Quote 7: "Sweat is just fat crying." - Unknown

This funny quote is a great reminder that working out can be tough, but it's worth it. It's a reminder that we're not just sweating, we're working towards our goals.


Quote 8: "I have a love-hate relationship with burpees. I love to hate them." - Unknown

Burpees are a tough exercise, but they're also a great way to get in shape. This quote is a reminder that sometimes we have to embrace the things we hate in order to achieve our goals.


Quote 9: "I work out because I really, really, really like food." - Unknown

This funny quote is perfect for those who love to eat. It's a reminder that working out can help us enjoy our favorite foods without feeling guilty.


Quote 10: "I work out because I want to look good naked. Well, at least with the lights off." - Unknown

This funny quote is perfect for those who are working towards their fitness goals. It's a reminder that we all have our reasons for working out, and they don't have to be serious.


Conclusion

Getting up early in the morning to work out can be tough, but it's worth it. These funny early morning workout quotes are a great way to get motivated and start your day off on the right foot. Whether you're a morning person or not, you can still make time for your workouts and achieve your fitness goals.
