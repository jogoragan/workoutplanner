Best Workout Apps For Pcos Your Solution To A Healthy Lifestyle

Written by April Jun 02, 2023 · 4 min read

.

Are you tired of trying different workout routines and diets to manage your PCOS symptoms? Do you struggle to stay motivated and consistent with your fitness goals? If yes, then you are not alone. Many women with PCOS face similar challenges. However, with the advancement in technology, you can now access various workout apps designed to help you manage your PCOS symptoms and achieve your fitness goals. In this article, we will discuss the best workout apps for PCOS and how they can benefit you.

1. FitOn

FitOn is an excellent workout app that offers a variety of workouts, including cardio, strength, yoga, and Pilates. The app also provides personalized meal plans to help you reach your fitness goals. FitOn has a community of women with PCOS who share their success stories and offer support to others. The app is free to download, and you can access most of its features without paying a dime. However, if you want access to more advanced features, you can subscribe to the premium version for $14.99 per month.

Pros

  • Provides personalized meal plans
  • Offers a variety of workouts
  • Has a supportive community
  • Free to download

Cons

  • The premium version is a bit expensive
  • Some users reported technical glitches

2. MyFitnessPal

MyFitnessPal is a popular app that helps you track your calories, macronutrients, and micronutrients. The app has a database of over six million foods, making it easy for you to log your meals. MyFitnessPal also integrates with other fitness apps, such as Fitbit and Apple Health. The app is free to download, but you can upgrade to the premium version for $9.99 per month.

Pros

  • Helps track your calorie intake
  • Large database of foods
  • Integrates with other fitness apps
  • Free to download

Cons

  • Some users reported inaccurate food data
  • The premium version is a bit expensive

3. SWEAT

SWEAT is a workout app designed specifically for women. The app offers various workout programs, including weight training, yoga, and Pilates. SWEAT also provides personalized meal plans and a supportive community of women. The app is free to download, but you can subscribe to the premium version for $19.99 per month.

Pros

  • Designed specifically for women
  • Offers various workout programs
  • Provides personalized meal plans
  • Has a supportive community

Cons

  • The premium version is a bit expensive
  • Some users reported technical glitches

4. Yoga for PCOS

If you prefer yoga, then the Yoga for PCOS app is an excellent option for you. The app offers various yoga poses designed to help manage PCOS symptoms. The app also provides detailed instructions on how to perform each pose correctly. Yoga for PCOS is free to download, but you can upgrade to the premium version for $7.99 per month.

Pros

  • Offers various yoga poses designed for PCOS
  • Provides detailed instructions
  • Free to download

Cons

  • The premium version is a bit expensive
  • Some users reported technical glitches

5. Nike Training Club

Nike Training Club is an excellent workout app that offers various workouts, including strength, endurance, and yoga. The app provides personalized workout plans based on your fitness goals and fitness level. Nike Training Club also has a community of women with PCOS who share their fitness journey. The app is free to download, and you can access most of its features without paying a dime.

Pros

  • Offers a variety of workouts
  • Provides personalized workout plans
  • Has a supportive community
  • Free to download

Cons

  • Some users reported technical glitches
  • The premium version is a bit expensive

Tips for Choosing the Best Workout App for PCOS

When choosing a workout app for PCOS, consider the following:
  • Look for an app that offers a variety of workouts
  • Choose an app that provides personalized meal plans
  • Read reviews from other users
  • Consider the app's user interface and ease of use
  • Choose an app that has a supportive community

FAQs

1. Can workout apps help manage PCOS symptoms?

Yes, workout apps can help manage PCOS symptoms by providing personalized workout plans and meal plans.

2. Are workout apps for PCOS free?

Most workout apps for PCOS offer a free version, but you can upgrade to the premium version for more advanced features.

3. Do I need gym equipment for these workout apps?

Most workout apps offer workouts that require little to no gym equipment. However, some workouts may require dumbbells or resistance bands.

Conclusion

In conclusion, workout apps can help manage PCOS symptoms and improve overall health and fitness. The best workout apps for PCOS are FitOn, MyFitnessPal, SWEAT, Yoga for PCOS, and Nike Training Club. When choosing a workout app, consider the app's features, user interface, and community. Remember, consistency is key when it comes to achieving your fitness goals, so choose an app that motivates and inspires you to stay committed to your fitness journey.
