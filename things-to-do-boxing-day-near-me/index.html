Boxing Day is a day to relax and enjoy time with family and friends. If you're wondering what to do on Boxing Day in 2023, you've come to the right place. In this guide, we'll explore some of the best things to do on Boxing Day near you. Whether you're looking for outdoor activities, shopping, or entertainment, we've got you covered. Let's get started!

1. Take a Winter Hike

If you're looking to get some fresh air and exercise, consider taking a winter hike. Many parks and trails are open on Boxing Day, and there's nothing quite like a crisp winter walk to clear your mind. Just be sure to dress warmly and bring along some snacks and water.

Tips:

  • Check the weather forecast before heading out.
  • Choose a trail that's suitable for winter hiking.
  • Bring along a map or GPS device.

2. Go Ice Skating

Another fun activity to do on Boxing Day is ice skating. Many cities have outdoor rinks that are open during the winter months, and skating can be a great way to burn off some holiday calories. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced skater, there's something magical about gliding across the ice.

Tips:

  • Wear warm clothing and gloves.
  • Bring along some hot cocoa or tea to enjoy after skating.
  • Check the rink's hours and admission fees before heading out.

3. Shop the Boxing Day Sales

If you're a bargain hunter, you won't want to miss the Boxing Day sales. Many retailers offer deep discounts on December 26th, and it's a great opportunity to snag some deals on holiday gifts or items for yourself. Just be prepared for crowds and long lines!

Tips:

  • Make a list of items you're looking for before heading out.
  • Check store hours and locations in advance.
  • Bring along a shopping buddy for moral support.

4. Have a Movie Marathon

If you'd rather stay cozy indoors, consider having a movie marathon on Boxing Day. There are plenty of holiday classics to choose from, or you can catch up on your favorite TV shows. Just be sure to have plenty of snacks and blankets on hand!

Tips:

  • Choose movies that everyone will enjoy.
  • Take breaks between movies to stretch your legs.
  • Make popcorn or other snacks to enjoy during the movies.

5. Volunteer in Your Community

Boxing Day is also a great day to give back to your community. Many organizations and charities are in need of volunteers during the holiday season, and helping others can be a rewarding way to spend the day. Whether you're serving at a soup kitchen, delivering meals, or helping with a toy drive, your efforts will be appreciated.

Tips:

  • Choose a volunteer opportunity that aligns with your interests and skills.
  • Sign up in advance to ensure a spot.
  • Dress appropriately for the activity (e.g. wear comfortable shoes for standing).

FAQs

Q: Are stores open on Boxing Day?

A: Yes, many stores are open on Boxing Day for the purpose of the Boxing Day sales.

Q: What time do stores open on Boxing Day?

A: Store hours vary, but many retailers open early (e.g. 6am) on Boxing Day.

Q: Can I return items purchased on Boxing Day?

A: Yes, most stores will honor returns and exchanges for items purchased on Boxing Day, although some may have different policies.

Pros and Cons of Boxing Day Activities

Like any activity, there are pros and cons to the options listed above. Here are a few to consider:

Winter Hike

Pros: Fresh air, exercise, beautiful scenery.

Cons: Cold weather, potential for icy conditions.

Ice Skating

Pros: Fun activity, great exercise, festive atmosphere.

Cons: Crowds, potential for injuries.

Boxing Day Sales

Pros: Deep discounts, fun shopping experience.

Cons: Crowds, long lines, potential for overspending.

Movie Marathon

Pros: Cozy atmosphere, time with loved ones, opportunity to relax.

Cons: Sedentary activity, potential for boredom.

Volunteering

Pros: Helping others, rewarding experience, opportunity to make a difference.

Cons: Potential for physical or emotional strain, time commitment.

Whatever you choose to do on Boxing Day, we hope you have a wonderful time with family and friends. Happy holidays!
