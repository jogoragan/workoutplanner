Arm Circles

This exercise is great for toning your shoulders and biceps. To perform arm circles, stand with your feet shoulder-width apart and extend your arms out to the sides. Slowly make circles with your arms, starting with small circles and gradually increasing the size. Repeat for 30 seconds, then switch directions and repeat for another 30 seconds.

Tip:

To make this exercise more challenging, hold a light weight in each hand while performing the arm circles.
