Are you looking for some fun and exciting activities to do on Christmas Day in Nashville? Look no further! We have created a list of the top things to do in Nashville that will make your holiday season unforgettable. From light displays to ice-skating, there is something for everyone. Let's dive into the details!

1. Visit the Gaylord Opryland Resort

The Gaylord Opryland Resort is known for its stunning Christmas decorations and holiday events. Walk through the resort's indoor gardens and enjoy the twinkling lights and festive music. Be sure to check out their ice-skating rink, snow tubing, and other holiday activities.

2. Explore the Cheekwood Estate & Gardens

Experience the magic of the season at the Cheekwood Estate & Gardens. The estate is transformed into a winter wonderland with beautiful holiday lights, carolers, and seasonal treats. Take a stroll through the gardens and enjoy the breathtaking scenery.

3. Take a Carriage Ride

Take a carriage ride through downtown Nashville and enjoy the sights and sounds of the city. It's the perfect way to get into the holiday spirit while taking in the beautiful scenery.

4. Visit the Nashville Zoo

The Nashville Zoo offers a unique holiday experience with their annual "Zoolumination" event. Walk through the zoo and enjoy the beautiful light displays while sipping on hot cocoa. There are also animal encounters and other holiday activities.

5. Attend a Christmas Concert

Nashville is known as the "Music City" for a reason. Attend a Christmas concert and enjoy the sounds of the season. Many venues offer holiday-themed shows, so be sure to check out their schedules.

6. Go Ice Skating

What better way to get into the holiday spirit than by ice skating? The Ford Ice Center and the Gaylord Opryland Resort both offer ice-skating rinks. Grab your skates and hit the ice for some festive fun.

7. Drive Through Christmas Lights

Take a drive through some of Nashville's most beautiful neighborhoods and enjoy the Christmas lights. Be sure to check out the "Festival of Lights" at the James E. Ward Agricultural Center in Lebanon, TN.

8. Volunteer

The holiday season is all about giving back. Volunteer at a local shelter, food bank, or hospital. Spread some holiday cheer and make a difference in someone's life.

9. Watch a Christmas Movie

Cuddle up on the couch and watch a classic Christmas movie. It's the perfect way to relax and unwind after a busy holiday season.

10. Have a Game Night

Gather your friends and family and have a game night. Play some holiday-themed games, like Christmas trivia or "Secret Santa". It's a fun and festive way to spend the holiday.

Final Thoughts

There you have it, folks - our top ten things to do on Christmas Day in Nashville. Whether you're looking for something adventurous or relaxing, there is something for everyone. Make this holiday season one to remember with these fun and festive activities. Happy Holidays!
