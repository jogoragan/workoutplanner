What To Do For A Day In Vegas

Written by Wednesday Jun 19, 2023 · 3 min read

.

If you're planning a trip to Las Vegas and only have a day to spend, don't worry! There's plenty to see and do in this exciting city. In this article, we'll provide you with a step-by-step guide on what to do for a day in Vegas.

Table of Contents

  • Morning
  • Afternoon
  • Evening
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Pros and Cons

Morning

Start your day with a hearty breakfast at one of the many restaurants on the Las Vegas Strip. The Strip is home to some of the best breakfast spots in the city. We recommend trying out Hash House A Go Go or Eggslut for a delicious start to your day. After breakfast, head out to the Grand Canyon for a thrilling helicopter tour. This tour will give you an amazing view of one of the most iconic natural wonders of the world. You'll be able to see the canyon from a completely different perspective and take some incredible photos.

Afternoon

After your helicopter tour, head back to the Strip for some shopping and sightseeing. The Strip is lined with some of the best shopping malls in the world, including the Forum Shops at Caesars Palace and the Miracle Mile Shops at Planet Hollywood. If you're interested in art, check out the Bellagio Gallery of Fine Art. The gallery features works from some of the most renowned artists in the world.

Evening

As the sun begins to set, head out to the High Roller Observation Wheel for an amazing view of the city. The High Roller is the tallest observation wheel in the world and provides a stunning view of the Las Vegas Strip. After your ride on the High Roller, check out one of the many shows that Las Vegas is famous for. Whether you're into magic, music, or comedy, there's a show for everyone in this city.

Tips

  • Plan your day in advance to make the most of your time in Vegas.
  • Wear comfortable shoes as you'll be doing a lot of walking.
  • Bring sunscreen and sunglasses as it can get very hot in Vegas.
  • Book your helicopter tour and show tickets in advance to avoid disappointment.

FAQ

What is the best time of year to visit Las Vegas?

The best time to visit Las Vegas is between March and May or between September and November. During these months, the weather is pleasant and there are fewer crowds.

What is the legal gambling age in Las Vegas?

The legal gambling age in Las Vegas is 21.

Pros and Cons

Pros

  • There's plenty to see and do in Las Vegas, even if you only have a day to spend.
  • The city is home to some of the best restaurants, shopping malls, and shows in the world.
  • The Grand Canyon helicopter tour and the High Roller Observation Wheel provide amazing views of the city and the natural wonders around it.

Cons

  • Vegas can be quite expensive, especially if you're staying on the Strip.
  • The city can be quite crowded, especially during peak season.
  • The heat in Vegas can be unbearable, especially during the summer months.
In conclusion, Las Vegas is a city that offers something for everyone. Whether you're interested in shopping, sightseeing, or just having a good time, you're sure to find it in this vibrant city. So, plan your day in advance, wear comfortable shoes, and get ready to have the time of your life!
