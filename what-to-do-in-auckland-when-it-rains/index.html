What To Do In Auckland When It Rains A Guide To Indoor Activities

Written by Bowie Dec 26, 2022 · 4 min read

When planning a trip to Auckland, it's important to remember that New Zealand's weather can be unpredictable. But don't let a rainy day ruin your plans! There are plenty of fun and exciting indoor activities to enjoy in Auckland when it rains. In this guide, we'll share our top picks for things to do when the weather outside is less than ideal.

How to Make the Most of Rainy Days in Auckland

When it comes to rainy day activities, Auckland has something for everyone. Whether you're a history buff, an art lover, or just looking for a fun way to stay dry, there are plenty of options to choose from. Here are some of our top picks:

Visit a Museum or Gallery

Auckland is home to a variety of world-class museums and galleries. The Auckland Art Gallery Toi o Tāmaki is a must-see for art lovers, with its impressive collection of New Zealand and international art. The Auckland War Memorial Museum is another popular choice, with exhibits showcasing the history and culture of New Zealand.

Explore the City's Indoor Markets

One of the best ways to experience Auckland's vibrant food and culture scene is by visiting one of the city's indoor markets. The La Cigale French Market is a popular choice, with its artisanal food stalls and live music. The Auckland Fish Market is another great option, with its fresh seafood and waterfront views.

Catch a Movie or Live Performance

There's nothing like escaping the rain with a good movie or live performance. Auckland has plenty of options to choose from, whether you're in the mood for a blockbuster movie at a mainstream cinema or a more indie film at a local theatre. The Civic Theatre is a stunning historic venue that hosts a variety of live performances and events.

Step-by-Step Guide to Planning Your Auckland Rainy Day Itinerary

Now that you have some ideas for indoor activities to enjoy in Auckland when it rains, it's time to start planning your itinerary. Here's a step-by-step guide:

Step 1: Check the Weather Forecast

Before you start planning your rainy day itinerary, be sure to check the weather forecast. This will help you determine how much time you'll need to spend indoors and which activities are best suited for the forecasted conditions.

Step 2: Choose Your Activities

Based on the weather forecast and your personal interests, choose which indoor activities you'll want to include in your itinerary. Be sure to research opening hours, admission prices, and any other important details ahead of time.

Step 3: Plan Your Transportation

Plan how you'll get to and from each activity on your itinerary. Auckland has a variety of transportation options, including public transit, taxis, and ride-sharing services like Uber.

Step 4: Plan Your Meals

Don't forget to plan your meals! Whether you're looking for a quick bite or a leisurely meal, Auckland has plenty of options to choose from. Research restaurants and cafes near your planned activities and make reservations if necessary.

Tips for Enjoying Your Auckland Rainy Day Itinerary

Now that you have your itinerary planned, here are some tips for making the most of your rainy day in Auckland:

Tip 1: Dress for the Weather

Be sure to dress appropriately for the weather. Auckland can be chilly and damp on rainy days, so bring a warm jacket and waterproof shoes.

Tip 2: Stay Flexible

Remember that the weather can be unpredictable, so be prepared to adjust your itinerary if necessary. Have a backup plan in case one of your planned activities is closed or cancelled due to weather conditions.

Tip 3: Embrace the Rain

Finally, don't let the rain get you down! Embrace the opportunity to experience Auckland's indoor attractions and enjoy the unique atmosphere of the city on a rainy day.

FAQ

What are some indoor activities to do in Auckland when it rains?

Some popular indoor activities to enjoy in Auckland when it rains include visiting museums and galleries, exploring indoor markets, catching a movie or live performance, and trying out indoor sports like rock climbing or trampolining.

What should I wear on a rainy day in Auckland?

Be sure to dress for the weather with a warm jacket and waterproof shoes. Auckland can be chilly and damp on rainy days.

How can I plan my Auckland rainy day itinerary?

Plan your Auckland rainy day itinerary by checking the weather forecast, choosing your activities, planning your transportation, and planning your meals. Remember to stay flexible and embrace the opportunity to experience Auckland's indoor attractions.

Pros and Cons of Visiting Auckland on a Rainy Day

Pros:

  • Less Crowded Attractions
  • Unique Atmosphere
  • Opportunities to Explore Indoor Activities

Cons:

  • Potentially Cold and Damp Weather
  • Possible Closures or Cancellations of Outdoor Activities
  • Less Time Spent Outdoors

Overall, visiting Auckland on a rainy day can be a unique and enjoyable experience. By embracing the weather and exploring indoor activities, you can make the most of your time in this vibrant city.
