Are you looking for a fun way to spend your Labor Day weekend in Indianapolis this year? Look no further! We’ve compiled a list of the best events happening in the city over the long weekend. From festivals to concerts, there’s something for everyone to enjoy. Keep reading to find out more!

Indy Jazz Fest

The Indy Jazz Fest is an annual celebration of all things jazz. This year, the festival will take place over Labor Day weekend, from September 1st to September 4th. The event features a lineup of local and national jazz musicians, as well as food and drink vendors. It’s the perfect way to spend a relaxing afternoon or evening enjoying great music and delicious food.

Location:

The Jazz Kitchen
5377 N College Ave
Indianapolis, IN 46220

Admission:

Tickets start at $25 per person.

Indiana State Fair

The Indiana State Fair is one of the biggest events of the year in Indianapolis. This year’s fair will run from August 4th to August 20th, and will include a variety of activities and events over Labor Day weekend. From food vendors to carnival rides to live music performances, there’s something for everyone at the Indiana State Fair.

Location:

Indiana State Fairgrounds
1202 E 38th St
Indianapolis, IN 46205

Admission:

Tickets start at $13 per person.

Indiana Brewers' Cup

The Indiana Brewers' Cup is an annual beer competition that takes place over Labor Day weekend. This year’s event will be held on September 2nd and 3rd, and will feature over 100 different types of beer from breweries across Indiana. In addition to tasting beer, attendees can also enjoy food trucks and live music.

Location:

Indiana State Fairgrounds
1202 E 38th St
Indianapolis, IN 46205

Admission:

Tickets start at $40 per person.

Monumental Marathon

The Monumental Marathon is one of the biggest races of the year in Indianapolis. This year’s event will take place on November 4th, but registration opens on Labor Day weekend. If you’re a runner or just want to cheer on the participants, the Monumental Marathon is a great way to spend your long weekend.

Location:

Downtown Indianapolis

Admission:

Registration fees vary depending on the race.

Indianapolis Colts Game

If you’re a sports fan, you won’t want to miss the Indianapolis Colts game over Labor Day weekend. This year’s game will be held on September 3rd at Lucas Oil Stadium. The Colts will be playing against the Cincinnati Bengals, so it’s sure to be an exciting game.

Location:

Lucas Oil Stadium
500 S Capitol Ave
Indianapolis, IN 46225

Admission:

Tickets start at $40 per person.

Tips for Enjoying Labor Day Weekend in Indianapolis

Here are a few tips to help you make the most of your Labor Day weekend in Indianapolis:

  • Buy tickets in advance for any events to avoid long lines and sold-out shows.
  • Wear comfortable shoes and clothing, especially if you plan on doing a lot of walking or standing.
  • Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water throughout the day.
  • Bring sunscreen and a hat to protect yourself from the sun.
  • Plan your transportation in advance, especially if you’re attending an event in a busy area or downtown.

Conclusion

From music festivals to beer competitions to sports games, there’s no shortage of things to do in Indianapolis over Labor Day weekend. Whether you’re a local or just visiting the city, be sure to check out some of these great events and make the most of your long weekend.
