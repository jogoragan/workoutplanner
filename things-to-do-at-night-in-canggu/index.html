Things To Do At Night In Canggu A Guide To Relaxing Evening Activities

In Canggu, Bali, the fun doesn't stop when the sun goes down. This trendy and laid-back beach town offers plenty of activities to keep you entertained after dark. Whether you're looking for a night out on the town or a more low-key evening, Canggu has something for everyone. In this guide, we'll explore the best things to do at night in Canggu in 2023.

Hit up the Beach Bars

One of the best things to do at night in Canggu is to hit up the beach bars. There are plenty of bars along the beach that offer stunning sunset views and delicious cocktails. Some of the most popular beach bars include Old Man's, The Lawn, and La Brisa. These bars often have live music or DJs playing, making them the perfect spot to dance the night away.

Explore the Night Markets

Canggu is known for its vibrant night markets, where you can find everything from street food to handmade crafts. The most popular night markets are the Love Anchor Canggu Market and the Samadi Sunday Market. These markets are a great place to grab a bite to eat, shop for souvenirs, or just soak up the lively atmosphere.

Catch Live Music

If you're a music lover, Canggu has plenty of options for catching live performances. Some of the best venues for live music include Deus Ex Machina and Gimme Shelter. These bars often have local bands or DJs playing, as well as occasional international acts.

Take a Yoga Class

Canggu is a hub for yoga enthusiasts, and there are plenty of studios offering evening classes. Some of the most popular studios include The Practice and Serenity Eco Guesthouse. Taking a yoga class is a great way to unwind and relax after a long day of exploring.

Enjoy a Movie Night

If you're looking for a more low-key evening activity, consider catching a movie at The Roxy Canggu. This retro-style cinema screens a mix of classic and contemporary films, as well as documentaries and indie flicks. The theater also has a bar and restaurant, making it the perfect spot for a cozy date night.

Indulge in Spa Treatments

Canggu is home to some of Bali's best spas, and many of them offer evening appointments. Treat yourself to a massage, facial, or other spa treatment at a luxurious spa such as Amo Spa or Spring Spa. With soothing music and relaxing scents, you're sure to leave feeling rejuvenated.

Eat Street Food

Canggu is a foodie's paradise, and there are plenty of street food stalls offering delicious and affordable eats. Some of the most popular street food spots include the Batu Bolong Night Market and the Berawa Night Market. Whether you're in the mood for Indonesian classics like nasi goreng or international favorites like tacos, you're sure to find something to satisfy your cravings.

Party at a Beach Club

If you're in the mood for a night out, Canggu has plenty of beach clubs that offer lively party scenes. Some of the most popular beach clubs include Finn's Beach Club and Cafe Del Mar. With DJs spinning the latest hits and plenty of drinks flowing, you're sure to have a night to remember.

Watch the Sunset

Canggu is known for its stunning sunsets, and there are plenty of spots to catch the nightly show. Whether you're watching from the beach, a rooftop bar, or your villa, there's nothing quite like the colors of the sky as the sun sinks below the horizon.

Go Night Surfing

Canggu is a popular surf destination, and many surfers enjoy hitting the waves after dark. While it's not recommended for beginners, experienced surfers can rent a board and hit the waves under the moonlight. Just be sure to stay safe and aware of your surroundings.

Tips for a Great Night Out in Canggu

  • Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water throughout the night.
  • Bring cash, as many places do not accept credit cards.
  • Wear comfortable shoes if you plan on dancing or walking around.
  • Be respectful of local customs and dress appropriately.

FAQs

Q: Is Canggu safe at night?

A: Canggu is generally a safe area, but it's always a good idea to be aware of your surroundings and stay in well-lit areas.

Q: What is the dress code for nightlife in Canggu?

A: Dress codes vary depending on the venue, but it's generally best to dress casually and comfortably.

Pros and Cons of Nightlife in Canggu

Pros:

  • Plenty of options for all types of travelers
  • Lively atmosphere and friendly locals
  • Beautiful scenery and stunning sunsets

Cons:

  • Some areas can be crowded and noisy
  • Not all venues accept credit cards
  • Alcohol can be expensive in some places

Overall, Canggu offers a diverse and exciting nightlife scene that is sure to appeal to all types of travelers. From beach bars to night markets, there's always something to do after dark in this vibrant town.
