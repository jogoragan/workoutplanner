3 Day Full Body Bulking Workout How To Build Muscle

If you're looking to pack on some muscle, a 3 day full body bulking workout might be just what you need. This workout focuses on targeting all major muscle groups in your body in just three days, allowing for ample recovery time in between. In this article, we'll break down exactly what a full body bulking workout entails and provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to execute it.

How to:

Before you get started, it's essential to understand the basic principles of bulking. Bulking is the process of increasing muscle mass by consuming more calories than you burn. To support muscle growth, you'll need to consume a diet high in protein and carbohydrates.

Now, let's dive into the workout. The 3 day full body bulking workout consists of three full-body workouts per week, with rest days in between. Each workout should be focused on compound exercises that target multiple muscle groups at once. Here's an example workout plan:

Day 1: Upper Body

  • Bench Press (3 sets of 8-10 reps)
  • Barbell Row (3 sets of 8-10 reps)
  • Shoulder Press (3 sets of 8-10 reps)
  • Pull-Ups (3 sets of 8-10 reps)
  • Bicep Curls (3 sets of 8-10 reps)

Day 2: Lower Body

  • Squats (3 sets of 8-10 reps)
  • Deadlifts (3 sets of 8-10 reps)
  • Leg Press (3 sets of 8-10 reps)
  • Lunges (3 sets of 8-10 reps per leg)
  • Calf Raises (3 sets of 8-10 reps)

Day 3: Full Body

  • Pull-Ups (3 sets of 8-10 reps)
  • Bench Press (3 sets of 8-10 reps)
  • Squats (3 sets of 8-10 reps)
  • Deadlifts (3 sets of 8-10 reps)
  • Shoulder Press (3 sets of 8-10 reps)
  • Bicep Curls (3 sets of 8-10 reps)
  • Leg Press (3 sets of 8-10 reps)
  • Calf Raises (3 sets of 8-10 reps)

Each exercise should be performed with proper form and technique. If you're unsure of how to perform an exercise, consult a personal trainer or fitness professional.

Tips:

Here are some additional tips to help you make the most of your 3 day full body bulking workout:

  • Gradually increase the weight you lift as you become stronger.
  • Make sure to get plenty of rest and recovery time in between workouts.
  • Stay hydrated and eat a balanced diet to support muscle growth.
  • Consider incorporating supplements like protein powder or creatine into your diet.

FAQ:

1. How long should I rest in between sets?

You should aim to rest for 60-90 seconds in between sets to allow your muscles time to recover.

2. How long will it take to see results?

Everyone's body is different, but with consistent effort and a proper diet, you should start to see results within a few weeks.

Pros and Cons:

Pros:

  • Efficient and time-effective workout plan
  • Focused on compound exercises for maximum muscle activation
  • Allows for ample recovery time in between workouts

Cons:

  • May not be suitable for beginners or those with limited gym experience
  • Requires a significant amount of effort and dedication to see results
  • May not be suitable for those with injuries or physical limitations

Overall, a 3 day full body bulking workout can be an effective way to build muscle and increase strength. However, it's important to approach this workout plan with caution and always consult a professional if you're unsure of how to perform an exercise. With hard work and dedication, you can achieve your fitness goals and see the results you desire.
