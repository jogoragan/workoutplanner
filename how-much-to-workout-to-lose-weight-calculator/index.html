FAQ

What is a healthy amount of weight to lose per week?

A healthy amount of weight to lose per week is 1-2 pounds. Losing weight at a slower pace can help ensure that the weight loss is sustainable and that you are not losing muscle mass.

Can you lose weight without exercise?

Yes, it is possible to lose weight without exercise by following a healthy diet and reducing your calorie intake. However, exercise can help speed up the weight loss process and has numerous other health benefits.

How often should I exercise to lose weight?

It is recommended that adults get at least 150 minutes of moderate-intensity exercise per week to maintain good health. To lose weight, you may need to increase this amount or incorporate more intense exercise into your routine.
