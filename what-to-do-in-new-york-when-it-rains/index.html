What To Do In New York When It Rains

When you plan a trip to New York City, you always hope for sunny weather. However, sometimes Mother Nature has other plans and the rain starts pouring. But don't worry, there are still plenty of things to do in the city that never sleeps, even when it's raining. Here are some tips and ideas for what to do in New York when it rains.

How to Prepare for the Rainy Day

Before we dive into the activities, it's important to prepare for the rainy day. Check the weather forecast and bring appropriate rain gear such as an umbrella, raincoat, and waterproof shoes. You can also buy these items at stores around the city if you forget to pack them. If you don't want to carry an umbrella, you can always buy a cheap one from street vendors in case of emergency.

Indoor Activities

Visit a Museum

New York City is home to some of the world's best museums, and they are the perfect place to spend a rainy day. Some of the most popular museums include the Metropolitan Museum of Art, the American Museum of Natural History, and the Museum of Modern Art. These museums have extensive collections that can keep you occupied for hours.

Watch a Broadway Show

Another great indoor activity is watching a Broadway show. There are plenty of theaters around the city that offer a variety of shows, from classic musicals to modern plays. You can buy tickets online or at the box office, and there are often discounts available for same-day shows.

Go Shopping

New York City is known for its shopping, and there are plenty of indoor malls and department stores to explore. Some of the most famous shopping destinations include Macy's, Bloomingdale's, and the Shops at Columbus Circle. You can also check out the indoor markets such as Chelsea Market and the Union Square Greenmarket.

Outdoor Activities

Explore Central Park

Even when it's raining, Central Park is still a beautiful place to explore. Put on your rain gear and take a walk around the park, or rent a bike and ride through the scenic paths. You can also visit the Central Park Zoo or take a carriage ride through the park.

Visit the 9/11 Memorial

The 9/11 Memorial is an outdoor attraction that is still worth visiting in the rain. The memorial features two waterfalls that represent the Twin Towers and a museum that tells the story of the tragic events of 9/11. You can also take a guided tour of the area to learn more about the history and significance of the site.

Take a Food Tour

New York City is known for its diverse food scene, and there are plenty of food tours that can take you to different neighborhoods and restaurants. You can try everything from pizza to dim sum, and the tours are often led by knowledgeable guides who can share the history and culture behind each dish.

Tips for Enjoying the Rain

Don't let the rain ruin your day in New York City. Here are some tips for enjoying the rainy weather:

  • Wear comfortable and waterproof shoes to avoid slipping and sliding.
  • Bring a waterproof bag or cover for your phone and other electronics.
  • Take breaks in cafes or restaurants to warm up and dry off.
  • Embrace the rain and enjoy the unique atmosphere it creates in the city.

FAQ

What is the best museum to visit on a rainy day?

The best museum to visit on a rainy day depends on your interests, but some popular choices include the Metropolitan Museum of Art, the American Museum of Natural History, and the Museum of Modern Art.

Can you still walk around Central Park in the rain?

Yes, Central Park is still open to visitors in the rain. Just make sure to wear appropriate rain gear and be careful on slippery paths.

Pros and Cons of Rainy Days in New York

Pros:

  • Less crowded tourist attractions
  • Cooler temperatures for outdoor activities
  • The unique atmosphere created by the rain

Cons:

  • Wet and uncomfortable conditions
  • Possible delays or cancellations for outdoor activities
  • The risk of getting sick from the rain and cold temperatures

Despite the cons, rainy days in New York City can still be enjoyable with the right attitude and preparation. Follow these tips and ideas for what to do in New York when it rains, and you'll have a great time exploring the city even in the wet conditions.
