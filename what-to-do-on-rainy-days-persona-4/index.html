Rainy days can be boring, but not in Persona 4. With its unique gameplay and immersive story, there's always something to do in this game even when it's pouring outside. In this article, we'll give you some tips and tricks on what to do on rainy days Persona 4.

Read Books

On rainy days, you can spend your time reading books. Reading books can increase your character's stats and give you new skills. You can buy books from the bookstore in the shopping district or borrow them from the school library. Some books can only be obtained by completing quests or through social links.

Go Fishing

Persona 4 has a fishing mini-game that you can play on rainy days. You can fish at the Samegawa River or the Sea Guardian. Fishing can increase your character's diligence and give you rare items. You can buy bait from the fishing store in the shopping district or find them in the world.

Do Laundry

Doing laundry may not sound like a fun activity, but it can help you increase your character's diligence. You can do laundry at the laundromat in the shopping district. Doing laundry can also give you new outfits and accessories.

Visit Shops

On rainy days, you can visit shops in the shopping district. You can buy new weapons, armor, healing items, and accessories. Some shops offer discounts on rainy days, so it's a good time to stock up on supplies.

Study

Studying can increase your character's knowledge and give you new skills. You can study at the library or at home. Studying can also help you pass exams and improve your grades.

Watch Movies

You can watch movies at the theater in the shopping district. Watching movies can increase your character's courage and give you new skills. You can also invite your friends to watch movies with you, which can improve your social links.

Work on Social Links

Persona 4 is all about building relationships. On rainy days, you can work on your social links by hanging out with your friends or completing quests. Social links can give you new skills and abilities, as well as unlock new story events.

Cook

You can cook at home by using ingredients you find in the world or buy from the shopping district. Cooking can increase your character's courage and give you new skills. You can also invite your friends to taste your cooking, which can improve your social links.

Play Video Games

Persona 4 has a video game mini-game that you can play on rainy days. You can play at the arcade in the shopping district or at home. Playing video games can increase your character's understanding and give you rare items.

Train

You can train your character's stats by running, doing push-ups, or practicing your swings at the batting cage. Training can increase your character's strength and give you new skills. You can also invite your friends to train with you, which can improve your social links.

Conclusion

As you can see, there's a lot to do on rainy days Persona 4. You can read books, go fishing, do laundry, visit shops, study, watch movies, work on social links, cook, play video games, and train. Each activity can help you improve your character's stats and give you new skills. So, next time it rains, don't feel bored. Boot up Persona 4 and have fun!
