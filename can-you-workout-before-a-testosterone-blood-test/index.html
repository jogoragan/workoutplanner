Why Workout Before a Testosterone Blood Test?

There are several reasons why you may want to workout before a testosterone blood test. First, working out can increase your testosterone levels, which can help you get better results on the test. Preparation Before the Test

Before you take a testosterone blood test, there are a few things you need to do to prepare. First, you should talk to your doctor about any medications or supplements you are taking that could affect your hormone levels. You should also avoid eating or drinking anything for at least eight hours before the test, as this can affect your results. Finally, make sure you get a good night's sleep the night before the test, as lack of sleep can also affect your hormone levels.

How long before a testosterone blood test should I workout?

You should workout at least a few hours before your testosterone blood test to allow your hormone levels to return to normal.

No, you should not eat or drink anything other than water for at least eight hours before your testosterone blood test.
