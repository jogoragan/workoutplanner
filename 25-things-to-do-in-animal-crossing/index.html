Welcome to Animal Crossing, a life simulation game where you can build your own community, interact with animals, and enjoy the simple joys of island living. If you're new to the game, it can be overwhelming to know where to start. That's why we've put together a list of 25 things to do in Animal Crossing to help you get started. So, let's dive in!

1. Create Your Character

The first thing you'll do in Animal Crossing is create your character. You can choose your gender, skin color, hairstyle, and clothing. Don't worry if you don't like your character's appearance later on – you can change it at any time!


2. Explore Your Island

Once you've created your character, it's time to start exploring your island. Walk around and get a feel for the place. You'll find all sorts of things to do, from fishing to bug catching to fossil hunting.


3. Build Your Home

Tom Nook, the local raccoon, will offer you a home loan so you can build your own house. You can customize the layout, furniture, and decorations to your heart's content.


4. Meet Your Neighbors

You'll have a variety of animal neighbors living on your island. Take some time to talk to them and get to know them. They may give you gifts or ask for your help with tasks.


5. Fishing

Grab a fishing rod and head to the nearest body of water. You can catch a variety of fish, which you can either sell or donate to the museum.


6. Catch Bugs

Don't forget to catch some bugs while you're exploring your island. You can sell them or donate them to the museum.


7. Plant Flowers and Trees

Add some color to your island by planting flowers and trees. You can even crossbreed flowers to create new colors.


8. Decorate Your Island

Once you've collected some furniture and decorations, it's time to start decorating your island. Get creative and make it your own!


9. Visit Other Islands

You can use your Nook Miles to visit other islands and collect resources. You may even meet new animal friends!


10. Design Clothes

If you're feeling creative, you can design your own clothes with the Able Sisters. Show off your unique style!


11. Attend Events

Animal Crossing has a variety of events throughout the year, from fishing tournaments to holiday celebrations. Don't miss out!


12. Complete Tasks for Tom Nook

Tom Nook will offer you tasks to complete, such as collecting materials or building furniture. Complete them to earn rewards!


13. Collect Fossils

As you explore your island, you'll find fossils buried underground. Donate them to the museum to complete your collection.


14. Create Your Own Music

If you have a musical ear, you can create your own music with K.K. Slider. Play it on your island and impress your friends!


15. Go Swimming

Once you unlock the ability to swim, you can explore the ocean and catch new sea creatures.


16. Play Mini-Games with Friends

Animal Crossing has a variety of mini-games you can play with friends, from hide-and-seek to treasure hunts.


17. Buy and Sell Items

You can buy and sell items with other animal friends or at the market. Make some bells and grow your collection!


18. Upgrade Your Home

As you earn more bells, you can upgrade your home and add new rooms. Make it the home of your dreams!


19. Customize Your Furniture

You can customize your furniture with different designs and colors. Make it match your island's aesthetic!


20. Find and Collect Artwork

You can find and collect artwork to display in your home or donate to the museum. Just be careful – some pieces may be forgeries!


21. Celebrate Holidays

Animal Crossing has a variety of holidays throughout the year, from Halloween to Christmas. Decorate your island and celebrate with your friends!


22. Create Your Own Designs

You can create your own designs with the Pro Design tool. Make clothes, wallpaper, and more!


23. Discover Hidden Secrets

Animal Crossing is full of hidden secrets and easter eggs. Keep exploring and see what you can find!


24. Make Friends with Your Villagers

Take some time to talk to your animal neighbors and build relationships with them. You may even become best friends!


25. Enjoy the Simple Pleasures of Island Life

Most importantly, enjoy the simple pleasures of island life. Relax on the beach, watch the sunset, and take in the beauty of your island.


Conclusion

There you have it – 25 things to do in Animal Crossing. Whether you're a new player or a seasoned pro, there's always something to do on your island. So go out there and have fun!
