Things To Do On Valentine s Day In Miami A Guide To Celebrate Love

Written by April Feb 18, 2023 · 6 min read

.

Are you looking for a romantic getaway or a perfect date night on Valentine’s Day in Miami? Then you are in the right place. Miami is a city known for its beaches, fine dining, and nightlife, making it a perfect destination for lovebirds. In this guide, we will share some of the top things to do on Valentine’s Day in Miami, from romantic dinners to unique experiences, to make your day unforgettable.

  • Beach Experience
  • Couple Spa

  • Conclusion

Romantic Dinners

Miami is known for its diverse culinary scene, and Valentine’s Day is the perfect occasion to explore it. From seafood to Italian, the city offers a wide range of romantic dinner options. Some of the top picks include:

Jaya at The Setai

Jaya at The Setai offers a romantic ambiance with a fusion of Asian and European cuisine. The restaurant’s dim-lit setting and live jazz music create a perfect atmosphere for couples.

Casa Tua

Casa Tua is a charming Mediterranean-style villa that offers a romantic dining experience. The restaurant’s candlelit courtyard and the outdoor garden create a romantic ambiance.

Le Jardinier

Le Jardinier is a contemporary French restaurant located in the Miami Design District. The restaurant’s modern and chic decor, coupled with its world-class cuisine, make it a perfect spot for a romantic dinner.

Beach Experience

Miami is known for its beautiful beaches, and a beach experience can be a perfect way to spend Valentine’s Day. From romantic walks to sunset views, the beach offers endless possibilities. Some of the top beach experiences include:

South Beach

South Beach is known for its white sand beaches and turquoise waters. Couples can take a romantic walk along the beach, watch the sunset, or have a picnic.

Crandon Park Beach

Crandon Park Beach is a secluded beach with crystal clear waters and breathtaking views. Couples can rent a cabana, have a romantic picnic, or take a dip in the ocean.

Couple Spa

A couple’s spa experience can be a perfect way to unwind and relax on Valentine’s Day. Miami offers several world-class spas that offer couples massages and treatments. Some of the top couple spas include:

The Spa at Mandarin Oriental

The Spa at Mandarin Oriental offers a luxurious spa experience with breathtaking views of Biscayne Bay. Couples can enjoy a couples massage, sauna, or a dip in the infinity pool.

The Standard Spa

The Standard Spa offers a unique spa experience with outdoor soaking tubs and a mud lounge. Couples can indulge in a couples massage, facial, or a private yoga session.

Art Walk

Miami is known for its vibrant arts and culture scene, and Valentine’s Day can be a perfect opportunity to explore it. Couples can take a romantic stroll through the Wynwood Art District and explore the street art and galleries. Some of the top art walks include:

Wynwood Art Walk

Wynwood Art Walk is a monthly event that takes place on the second Saturday of every month. Couples can explore the street art, galleries, and enjoy live music and food trucks.

Art Deco District

The Art Deco District is a historic neighborhood in Miami Beach known for its pastel-colored buildings and architecture. Couples can take a romantic walk through the district and explore the art deco buildings and shops.

Sunset Cruise

A sunset cruise can be a perfect way to spend Valentine’s Day in Miami. Couples can enjoy breathtaking views of the city skyline and watch the sunset over the ocean. Some of the top sunset cruises include:

Biscayne Bay Sunset Cruise

The Biscayne Bay Sunset Cruise offers a romantic experience with live music, drinks, and hors d’oeuvres. Couples can enjoy views of the city skyline, Miami Beach, and the Port of Miami.

Island Queen Sunset Cruise

The Island Queen Sunset Cruise offers a relaxing experience with panoramic views of the city skyline and Miami Beach. Couples can enjoy a complimentary drink and unwind on the open-air deck.

Outdoor Activities

Miami offers several outdoor activities that couples can enjoy on Valentine’s Day. From kayaking to hiking, the city has something for every couple. Some of the top outdoor activities include:

Kayaking in Oleta River State Park

Oleta River State Park offers kayaking and paddleboarding through the mangrove forests and waterways. Couples can enjoy the scenic views and wildlife.

Biking on South Beach

Couples can rent bikes and explore the famous Ocean Drive and Art Deco District on South Beach. The bike ride offers a scenic view of the beach and the city skyline.

Live Music

Miami has a vibrant music scene with several live music venues that offer a romantic ambiance. Couples can enjoy live music performances and drinks on Valentine’s Day. Some of the top live music venues include:

Lagniappe

Lagniappe is a cozy wine bar in the Wynwood Arts District that offers live music performances and outdoor seating. Couples can enjoy a glass of wine and listen to live jazz, blues, and soul music.

Ball & Chain

Ball & Chain is a historic bar in Little Havana that offers live music performances and a dance floor. Couples can enjoy a night of salsa, jazz, and Afro-Cuban music.

Wine Tasting

Wine tasting can be a perfect way to celebrate love on Valentine’s Day. Miami offers several wine bars and wineries that offer wine tasting and food pairings. Some of the top wine tasting spots include:

Abaco Wines & Wine Bar

Abaco Wines & Wine Bar is a cozy wine bar in the South of Fifth neighborhood that offers a wide selection of wines and small plates. Couples can enjoy a wine flight and food pairing.

Schnebly Redland’s Winery

Schnebly Redland’s Winery is a winery located in the Redland farming area that offers wine tasting and tours. Couples can enjoy a wine flight and a tour of the winery.

Unique Experiences

Miami offers several unique experiences that couples can enjoy on Valentine’s Day. From private yacht charters to hot air balloon rides, the city has something for every couple. Some of the top unique experiences include:

Private Yacht Charter

Couples can rent a private yacht and explore the Biscayne Bay and the Miami skyline. The yacht charter can include a private chef, drinks, and a romantic sunset dinner.

Hot Air Balloon Ride

Couples can take a hot air balloon ride over the scenic views of Miami and the Everglades. The hot air balloon ride can include a champagne toast and a souvenir.

Conclusion

Miami offers endless possibilities for couples on Valentine’s Day. From romantic dinners to unique experiences, the city has something for every couple. We hope this guide has helped you plan your perfect Valentine’s Day in Miami. Celebrate love and create unforgettable memories with your significant other.
