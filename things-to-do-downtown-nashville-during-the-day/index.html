Things To Do Downtown Nashville During The Day

If you're looking for a fun and exciting way to spend your day in Nashville, look no further than downtown. With its vibrant music scene, delicious food, and unique attractions, there's something for everyone in this bustling part of the city. Here are some of the best things to do downtown Nashville during the day:

Visit the Country Music Hall of Fame

The Country Music Hall of Fame is a must-visit for music lovers. This museum showcases the history of country music, including exhibits on legendary performers like Johnny Cash and Dolly Parton. You can also take a tour of RCA Studio B, where many famous country hits were recorded.

Explore the Nashville Farmers' Market

The Nashville Farmers' Market is a great place to sample local produce and artisanal goods. With dozens of vendors selling everything from fresh fruits and vegetables to handmade crafts, there's plenty to see and do at this bustling market.

Take a Walk Along Broadway

Broadway is the heart of Nashville's music scene, with dozens of bars and clubs showcasing live performers throughout the day. Even if you're not a big fan of country music, you're sure to find something to enjoy on this lively street.

Visit the Ryman Auditorium

The Ryman Auditorium is one of Nashville's most iconic landmarks. This historic venue has hosted many famous performers over the years, including Elvis Presley and Hank Williams. Take a tour of the auditorium to learn more about its fascinating history.

Check Out the Parthenon

The Parthenon is a full-scale replica of the ancient Greek temple, located in the heart of Nashville's Centennial Park. This impressive structure is home to an art museum and a stunning statue of Athena, making it a must-see attraction for history buffs and art lovers alike.

Visit the Frist Art Museum

The Frist Art Museum is one of Nashville's premier cultural institutions, showcasing a wide variety of contemporary and classical art. With changing exhibits throughout the year, there's always something new to see at this impressive museum.

Take a Pedal Tavern Tour

Pedal tavern tours are a fun and unique way to explore downtown Nashville. These tours take you on a pub crawl through some of the city's best bars and clubs, all while pedaling on a custom-built bike. It's a great way to see the city while getting some exercise!

Enjoy Some Hot Chicken

Hot chicken is a Nashville specialty, and there are plenty of restaurants downtown that serve up this spicy dish. Whether you like it mild or extra hot, you're sure to find a restaurant that serves up the perfect plate of hot chicken.

Visit the Johnny Cash Museum

The Johnny Cash Museum is a must-visit for fans of the legendary musician. This museum features exhibits on Cash's life and career, including rare photos and artifacts. You can even see his famous black suit and guitar!

Take a Riverboat Cruise

A riverboat cruise along the Cumberland River is a great way to see Nashville from a different perspective. These cruises typically last a few hours and offer stunning views of the city's skyline and landmarks.

Conclusion

Whether you're a music lover, foodie, or history buff, downtown Nashville has something for everyone. From the Country Music Hall of Fame to hot chicken restaurants, there are plenty of ways to spend your day in this vibrant part of the city. So what are you waiting for? Start planning your trip to downtown Nashville today!
