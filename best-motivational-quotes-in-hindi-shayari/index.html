What is Hindi Shayari?

Hindi Shayari is a type of poetry that originated in India and is written in the Hindi language. It is a form of expression that has been used for centuries to convey thoughts, emotions, and feelings in a structured and lyrical manner. Hindi Shayari is typically composed of two or more lines and often rhymes. It covers a wide range of topics such as love, life, nature, and spirituality.
