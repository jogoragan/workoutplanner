Are you spending Christmas Day in London this year and looking for some relaxing and enjoyable activities to do? Look no further! We have compiled a list of things to do in Christmas Day London that will make your holiday a memorable one.

1. Visit Winter Wonderland

Winter Wonderland is a must-visit attraction during the Christmas season in London. You can enjoy festive food and drinks, take a ride on some of the attractions, and watch live entertainment. Admission to Winter Wonderland is free, but some of the attractions require tickets. Make sure to bundle up, as it can get cold!

2. Take a Christmas Day Walk

London is a beautiful city, and taking a walk on Christmas Day is a peaceful and enjoyable way to take it all in. Some great places to walk include Hyde Park, Regent's Park, and Hampstead Heath. Don't forget to bring your camera!

3. Have a Festive Meal

Many restaurants in London offer Christmas Day meals, so you don't have to worry about cooking. Make a reservation ahead of time, as they tend to fill up quickly. Some great places to consider include The Ivy, The Ritz, and Sketch.

4. Attend a Christmas Service

London has many beautiful churches and cathedrals that offer Christmas Day services. Some of the most popular include St. Paul's Cathedral, Westminster Abbey, and Southwark Cathedral. Make sure to arrive early, as they tend to fill up quickly.

5. Go Ice Skating

Nothing says Christmas like ice skating! London has many ice skating rinks that are open on Christmas Day, including Somerset House, Natural History Museum, and Canary Wharf. Make sure to bundle up and bring gloves!

6. Watch a Christmas Movie

If you're looking for a more relaxed activity, why not watch a Christmas movie? Many cinemas in London are open on Christmas Day, including Odeon, Vue, and Cineworld. Some great movies to consider include Love Actually, Home Alone, and It's A Wonderful Life.

7. Visit a Christmas Market

London has many Christmas markets that are open on Christmas Day, including the Southbank Centre's Winter Market, Leicester Square Christmas Market, and the Greenwich Market. You can find unique gifts, festive food and drinks, and live entertainment.

8. Go on a Christmas Lights Tour

London is known for its beautiful Christmas lights, and going on a Christmas lights tour is a great way to see them all. Some companies offer guided tours, but you can also create your own by walking around some of the popular areas, such as Oxford Street, Regent Street, and Covent Garden.

9. Take a Thames River Cruise

Seeing London from the Thames River is a unique and enjoyable experience. Many companies offer Christmas Day cruises, including Thames River Services, City Cruises, and London Party Boats. You can enjoy a festive meal and drinks while taking in the beautiful views.

10. Enjoy a Spa Day

If you're looking for a relaxing activity, why not treat yourself to a spa day? Many spas in London offer Christmas Day packages, including The Dorchester, The Bulgari Hotel, and The Mandarin Oriental. You can enjoy massages, facials, and other treatments to help you unwind.

Tips for Enjoying Christmas Day in London

- Plan ahead and make reservations for any activities or meals you want to do.
- Dress warmly, as it can get cold in London during the winter.
- Take advantage of public transportation, as some areas may be closed to cars on Christmas Day.
- Bring a camera to capture the beautiful sights.
- Don't forget to relax and enjoy the holiday!

Conclusion

There are many enjoyable and relaxing things to do in Christmas Day London. Whether you want to see the sights, enjoy a festive meal, or unwind at a spa, there is something for everyone. Make sure to plan ahead and enjoy the holiday!
