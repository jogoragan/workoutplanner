What To Do Christmas Day In Nashville A Guide To Relaxing Festivities

Welcome to the ultimate guide on how to spend Christmas Day in Nashville, Tennessee. Whether you are a resident or a visitor of the Music City, you will find plenty of options to celebrate this special day. From indulging in delicious food to enjoying festive events, Nashville has something for everyone. In this guide, we will walk you through some of the best things to do in Nashville on Christmas Day.

Table of Contents

  • Indulge in Delicious Food
  • Enjoy Festive Events
  • Relax and Unwind
  • Explore Nashville’s Attractions
  • Conclusion

Indulge in Delicious Food

One of the best things about Christmas Day is the food. Nashville has a variety of restaurants that are open on this day, offering mouth-watering dishes. Some of the best places to enjoy a festive meal include:

The Southern Steak & Oyster

This restaurant is located in the heart of downtown Nashville and serves up delicious Southern cuisine. On Christmas Day, they offer a special holiday menu that features traditional dishes such as roasted turkey and honey-glazed ham.

The Capitol Grille

If you’re looking for a more upscale dining experience, The Capitol Grille is the place to go. This restaurant is located inside the Hermitage Hotel and offers a four-course Christmas Day menu that includes dishes like lobster bisque and prime rib.

Enjoy Festive Events

Christmas Day is also a great time to enjoy festive events with family and friends. Here are some of the best events taking place in Nashville on this day:

Gaylord Opryland’s A Country Christmas

This event takes place at the Gaylord Opryland Resort and features a variety of holiday activities. From ice skating to gingerbread decorating, there is something for everyone. You can also enjoy a holiday lights display and a visit with Santa Claus.

Nashville Christmas Day Tour

This tour takes you around Nashville to see some of the city’s most famous holiday attractions. You will visit the Country Music Hall of Fame, the Ryman Auditorium, and the Parthenon, among other places.

Relax and Unwind

For those who prefer a more laid-back Christmas Day, Nashville has plenty of options for relaxation and unwinding. Here are some of the best things to do:

Visit a Spa

Nashville has a variety of spas that offer relaxing treatments such as massages and facials. Book yourself a spa day and unwind from the hustle and bustle of the holiday season.

Watch a Movie

There’s nothing like a good movie to help you relax. Nashville has several movie theaters that are open on Christmas Day, including the Regal Cinemas Opry Mills and the Belcourt Theatre.

Explore Nashville’s Attractions

Christmas Day is a great time to explore some of Nashville’s famous attractions. Here are some places to check out:

Country Music Hall of Fame

The Country Music Hall of Fame is a must-visit for music lovers. Explore the history of country music and see artifacts from some of the most famous musicians in the industry.

The Parthenon

The Parthenon is a full-scale replica of the original Parthenon in Athens, Greece. It is located in Centennial Park and is a great place to learn about Greek history and mythology.

Conclusion

Christmas Day in Nashville is a special time to celebrate with family and friends. Whether you prefer a festive event or a relaxing day, there is something for everyone in the Music City. We hope this guide has helped you plan your perfect Christmas Day in Nashville!
