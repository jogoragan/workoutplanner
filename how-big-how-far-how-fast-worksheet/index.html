What is the 'How Big How Far How Fast' Worksheet?

The 'how big how far how fast' is a worksheet that helps students understand the relationship between distance, speed, and time. It is an essential concept in physics that helps to calculate the speed of an object, the distance it covers, and the time taken to cover that distance. The worksheet is designed to help students visualize and comprehend the concept of distance, speed, and time, and how they are related.

How to Use the 'How Big How Far How Fast' Worksheet

The 'how big how far how fast' worksheet is easy to use, and with a little practice, you will be able to master it in no time. Here is how to use the worksheet:

Step 1: Identify the Variables

The first step in using the worksheet is to identify the variables. The variables in this case are distance, speed, and time. Once you have identified them, you can proceed to the next step.

Step 2: Determine the Formula

The next step is to determine the formula you will use to calculate the values of the variables. The formula for calculating the speed is:

Speed = Distance ÷ Time

The formula for calculating the distance is:

Distance = Speed x Time

The formula for calculating the time is:

Time = Distance ÷ Speed

Step 3: Solve the Problem

Once you have identified the variables and determined the formula, you can use the worksheet to solve the problem. Simply fill in the values of the variables in the appropriate spaces and use the formula to calculate the missing value. For example, if you want to calculate the speed of an object that traveled a distance of 100 meters in 10 seconds, you would fill in the values of distance and time and use the formula:

Speed = Distance ÷ Time

Speed = 100 meters ÷ 10 seconds = 10 meters per second
