Easy Meals for 12 Year Olds to Cook | Quick and Delicious Recipes

Are you a 12 year old looking to impress your family with your cooking skills? Or a parent looking for easy meals for your child to make? Look no further! We've put together a list of quick and delicious recipes that are perfect for young chefs. From breakfast to dinner, these meals are easy to make and sure to impress.

Table of Contents:

  1. Breakfast
  2. Lunch
  3. Dinner
  4. Dessert

Breakfast:

Start your day off right with these easy breakfast recipes:

Pancakes:

Pancakes are a classic breakfast food that are easy to make. All you need is pancake mix, milk, eggs, and oil. Mix the ingredients together in a bowl and pour onto a greased pan. Cook until golden brown on both sides and serve with syrup and butter.

Fruit Smoothie:

If you're in a hurry, a fruit smoothie is a quick and easy breakfast option. Blend together your favorite fruits, yogurt, and milk for a healthy and delicious breakfast.

Lunch:

Get creative with these easy lunch recipes:

Pizza:

Who doesn't love pizza? Buy pre-made pizza dough or use a tortilla wrap as a base. Add tomato sauce, cheese, and your favorite toppings. Bake in the oven for 10-15 minutes at 375°F.

Tuna Salad Sandwich:

For a quick and easy lunch, make a tuna salad sandwich. Mix canned tuna, mayonnaise, and chopped celery together. Serve on bread with lettuce and tomato.

Dinner:

Impress your family with these easy dinner recipes:

Spaghetti and Meatballs:

Cook spaghetti according to package instructions. For the meatballs, mix together ground beef, bread crumbs, egg, and Italian seasoning. Roll into balls and bake at 375°F for 20-25 minutes. Serve with tomato sauce and Parmesan cheese.

Tacos:

Who doesn't love tacos? Brown ground beef in a pan and add taco seasoning. Heat up taco shells in the oven and serve with beef, lettuce, tomato, cheese, and sour cream.

Dessert:

Satisfy your sweet tooth with these easy dessert recipes:

Chocolate Chip Cookies:

Mix together flour, sugar, butter, eggs, and chocolate chips. Spoon onto a greased baking sheet and bake in the oven at 375°F for 10-12 minutes.

Fruit Salad:

Cut up your favorite fruits and mix together in a bowl. Serve with whipped cream or yogurt.

Tips:

  • Always remember to wash your hands before cooking.
  • Read through the entire recipe before starting to ensure you have all the ingredients and equipment needed.
  • Always use oven mitts when handling hot pans or dishes.

FAQ:

What if I don't like the recipe?

That's okay! Experiment with different ingredients and find what works for you.

Can I make substitutions?

Absolutely! If you don't have a certain ingredient or don't like it, feel free to substitute it with something else.

Pros and Cons:

Pros:

  • Quick and easy recipes.
  • Perfect for young chefs.
  • Sure to impress family and friends.

Cons:

  • May require adult supervision.
  • May not be suitable for more advanced chefs.

Overall, these easy meals for 12 year olds to cook are perfect for young chefs looking to impress their family and friends. Whether you're making breakfast, lunch, dinner, or dessert, these recipes are sure to be a hit. Remember to follow the tips and have fun in the kitchen!
