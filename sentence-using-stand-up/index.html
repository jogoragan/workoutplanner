Definition of Stand Up

Stand up is a phrasal verb that means to rise from a sitting or lying position, to stand erect, or to move to an upright position. It is commonly used in everyday conversations, whether in formal or informal settings. Incorporating stand up in your sentences can make your speech more descriptive and interesting.

How to Use Stand Up in a Sentence

Here are some examples of how to use stand up in a sentence:

  • I always feel dizzy when I stand up too quickly.
  • Can you please stand up and let me sit down?
  • The comedian made us all stand up and dance to his funny song.
  • My favorite part of the concert was when the lead singer asked us to stand up and sing along.
  • During our meeting, the CEO asked us to stand up and introduce ourselves.
