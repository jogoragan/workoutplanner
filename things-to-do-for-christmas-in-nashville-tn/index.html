Are you looking for ways to celebrate Christmas in Nashville? Nashville, Tennessee, is a beautiful city that celebrates Christmas in style. The city is full of holiday cheer and has many activities and events that you can enjoy. In this blog post, we will discuss some of the best things to do for Christmas in Nashville TN.

Christmas Lights

One of the best things to do for Christmas in Nashville TN is to see the beautiful Christmas lights. Nashville has several places you can go to see the beautiful lights, such as the Cheekwood Estate and Gardens or the Opryland Hotel. The Opryland Hotel is known for its beautiful Christmas decorations and lights.

Christmas Music

Another great thing to do for Christmas in Nashville TN is to listen to Christmas music. Nashville is known as the Music City, so it’s no surprise that there are many Christmas music events throughout the city. You can attend the Nashville Christmas Parade, the Nashville Symphony Holiday Concert, or the Grand Ole Opry Christmas Show.

Christmas Markets

Christmas Markets are a great way to get into the holiday spirit. Nashville has several markets, such as the Nashville Christmas Village and the Holiday Market at the Nashville Farmers Market. These markets offer unique gifts, holiday decorations, and delicious food.

Christmas Parades

Attending a Christmas parade is a great way to celebrate the holiday season. Nashville has several parades, such as the Nashville Christmas Parade and the Franklin Christmas Parade. These parades have floats, music, and lots of holiday cheer.

Ice Skating

Ice skating is a fun holiday activity that the whole family can enjoy. Nashville has several ice skating rinks, such as the Ford Ice Center or the Ascend Amphitheater. These rinks offer public skating sessions and skate rentals.

Holiday Movies

Curling up with a holiday movie is a great way to get into the Christmas spirit. Nashville has several movie theaters that show holiday movies, such as the Belcourt Theatre or the Regal Opry Mills. You can also attend a holiday movie screening at the Country Music Hall of Fame.

Holiday Food

Food is a big part of the holiday season, and Nashville has many restaurants that offer delicious holiday-themed dishes. You can try the Christmas menu at the Loveless Cafe or the Christmas Eve Dinner at the Capitol Grille.

Holiday Beverages

Another way to get into the holiday spirit is to enjoy holiday-themed beverages. Nashville has several bars and coffee shops that offer holiday drinks, such as the Gingerbread Latte at Starbucks or the Hot Toddy at the Patterson House.

Holiday Shopping

Shopping for gifts is a big part of the holiday season, and Nashville has several shopping destinations. You can shop at the Mall at Green Hills, the Opry Mills Mall, or the Hill Center.

Holiday Charities

The holiday season is also a time to give back to the community. Nashville has many charities that you can donate to, such as the Second Harvest Food Bank or the Salvation Army.

Tips for Celebrating Christmas in Nashville TN

Here are some tips for celebrating Christmas in Nashville:

  • Check the event schedule beforehand to plan your activities.
  • Bundle up, as it can get chilly during the winter in Nashville.
  • Book your tickets early for popular events.
  • Try local holiday-themed food and drinks.
  • Support local businesses by shopping at local markets and stores.

FAQ

What are the best things to do for Christmas in Nashville TN?

The best things to do for Christmas in Nashville TN are seeing the Christmas lights, listening to Christmas music, attending Christmas markets and parades, ice skating, watching holiday movies, enjoying holiday-themed food and beverages, shopping for gifts, and donating to holiday charities.

Where can I see Christmas lights in Nashville TN?

You can see Christmas lights at the Cheekwood Estate and Gardens or the Opryland Hotel in Nashville TN.

What are some local holiday-themed food and drinks?

Some local holiday-themed food and drinks in Nashville TN are the Christmas menu at the Loveless Cafe, the Gingerbread Latte at Starbucks, and the Hot Toddy at the Patterson House.

How can I support local businesses during the holiday season in Nashville TN?

You can support local businesses by shopping at local markets and stores, such as the Nashville Christmas Village, the Holiday Market at the Nashville Farmers Market, the Mall at Green Hills, the Opry Mills Mall, or the Hill Center.

Pros and Cons of Celebrating Christmas in Nashville TN

Pros

  • Nashville has many holiday-themed activities and events.
  • The city is beautiful during the holiday season.
  • There are many unique holiday-themed food and drinks to try.
  • You can support local businesses by shopping at local markets and stores.

Cons

  • The weather can be chilly during the winter.
  • Popular events can get crowded and require early booking.
  • Some activities and events may require an entrance fee.

Now that you know some of the best things to do for Christmas in Nashville TN, plan your trip and enjoy the holiday season in Music City!
