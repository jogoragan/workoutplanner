Daytona Beach, FL, is a popular tourist destination known for its beautiful beaches, great weather, and exciting attractions. Whether you're looking for a relaxing day at the beach or a fun-filled adventure, Daytona Beach has something for everyone. In this article, we'll go over the top things to do in Daytona Beach, FL, to help you plan your next vacation.

Beaches

Daytona Beach is famous for its long stretches of beautiful beaches. Whether you're looking for a quiet spot to relax or a place to catch some waves, there's a beach for you in Daytona Beach. Some of the most popular beaches in Daytona Beach include:

Daytona Beach

Located in the heart of Daytona Beach, this beach is known for its wide stretches of sand and clear blue waters. It's a great spot for swimming, sunbathing, and surfing.

New Smyrna Beach

Just a short drive south of Daytona Beach, New Smyrna Beach is known for its laid-back vibe and great surf. It's a popular spot for locals and visitors alike.

Ormond Beach

Located just north of Daytona Beach, Ormond Beach is a quieter alternative to the hustle and bustle of Daytona Beach. It's a great spot for a relaxing day at the beach.

Attractions

Daytona Beach is home to a variety of attractions that are sure to keep you entertained. From museums to water parks, there's something for everyone. Some of the top attractions in Daytona Beach include:

Daytona International Speedway

Home to the Daytona 500, this iconic race track is a must-visit for any racing fan. You can take a tour of the track or even drive on it yourself.

Daytona Beach Boardwalk and Pier

This historic boardwalk is home to a variety of shops, restaurants, and attractions. Take a stroll down the boardwalk or enjoy a ride on the Ferris wheel.

Marine Science Center

Learn about the marine life that calls Florida home at this interactive museum. You can even touch a stingray or sea star.

Dining

Daytona Beach is home to a variety of restaurants that offer everything from seafood to pizza. Some of the top restaurants in Daytona Beach include:

Crabby Joe's Deck and Grill

Located on the Sunglow Pier, this restaurant offers stunning views of the ocean and delicious seafood dishes.

Ocean Deck Beach Club

This beachfront restaurant is known for its live music and tasty cocktails. It's a great spot to grab a bite to eat after a day at the beach.

Steve's Famous Diner

This classic diner is a must-visit for anyone looking for a hearty breakfast or lunch. Be sure to try their famous pancakes.

Shopping

Whether you're looking for souvenirs or high-end fashion, Daytona Beach has a variety of shopping options. Some of the top places to shop in Daytona Beach include:

One Daytona

This outdoor shopping mall is home to a variety of stores, restaurants, and entertainment options. It's a great spot to spend an afternoon.

Volusia Mall

Located in nearby Daytona Beach, this indoor mall offers a variety of stores, including Macy's and JCPenney.

Daytona Flea and Farmers Market

This outdoor market is a great place to find unique gifts and souvenirs. It's open every weekend and offers a variety of vendors.

Outdoor Activities

With its great weather and beautiful scenery, Daytona Beach is the perfect place to enjoy outdoor activities. Some of the top outdoor activities in Daytona Beach include:

Kayaking and Paddleboarding

Explore the waterways around Daytona Beach by renting a kayak or paddleboard. You can even take a guided tour.

Golfing

Daytona Beach is home to a variety of golf courses, including the LPGA International Golf Club and the Daytona Beach Golf Club.

Biking

Rent a bike and explore the scenic trails around Daytona Beach. You can even ride on the beach in some areas.

Events

Daytona Beach is home to a variety of events throughout the year. Some of the top events include:

Daytona 500

This iconic NASCAR race takes place every February at the Daytona International Speedway.

Biketoberfest

This annual motorcycle event takes place every October and features live music, vendors, and motorcycle shows.

Daytona Beach Bandshell Concert Series

Enjoy free concerts every Friday and Saturday night at the historic Daytona Beach Bandshell.

Accommodations

Daytona Beach offers a variety of accommodations, including hotels, motels, and vacation rentals. Some of the top places to stay in Daytona Beach include:

The Shores Resort and Spa

This beachfront resort offers luxurious accommodations and a variety of amenities, including a spa and on-site dining.

Daytona Beach Regency

This oceanfront resort offers spacious suites and a variety of amenities, including a pool and fitness center.

Wyndham Ocean Walk

This beachfront resort offers a variety of accommodations, including studios and suites. It's located near the Daytona Beach Boardwalk and Pier.

Transportation

Daytona Beach is easily accessible by car or plane. The Daytona Beach International Airport is located just a few miles from the beach, and several major highways run through the area. Once you're in Daytona Beach, you can get around by car, bike, or public transportation.

Tips

  • Be sure to pack sunscreen and a hat for a day at the beach.
  • If you're visiting during peak season, be prepared for crowds and traffic.
  • Consider renting a car if you plan on exploring the area.
  • Check the weather forecast before planning outdoor activities.

FAQs

What is the best time to visit Daytona Beach?

The best time to visit Daytona Beach is between March and May when the weather is warm and the crowds are smaller.

What is there to do in Daytona Beach besides going to the beach?

Daytona Beach is home to a variety of attractions, including the Daytona International Speedway, the Marine Science Center, and the Daytona Beach Boardwalk and Pier.

Is it possible to drive on the beach in Daytona Beach?

Yes, you can drive on certain sections of the beach in Daytona Beach. Be sure to check local regulations before you go.

Can you rent bikes in Daytona Beach?

Yes, there are several places to rent bikes in Daytona Beach. You can even rent bikes on the beach in some areas.

Whether you're looking for a relaxing day at the beach or an exciting adventure, Daytona Beach has something for everyone. Use this guide to plan your next vacation to Daytona Beach, FL.
