Description

Harrods high tea is a luxurious experience that takes place in the Georgian Restaurant, located on the fourth floor of the department store. The restaurant has a beautiful and elegant ambiance, with stunning views of the London skyline. The high tea experience includes a selection of delicious sandwiches, scones, cakes, and pastries, served with a choice of tea or coffee. The menu is changed seasonally, so visitors can expect to try new and exciting treats every time they visit.

How to

If you are interested in experiencing high tea at Harrods, there are a few things you need to know. Firstly, it is essential to book ahead of time, as the restaurant can get very busy. You can book online, over the phone, or in person at the restaurant. It is also worth noting that there is a dress code in place, so make sure to dress appropriately.

Step by Step

Here is a step by step guide on how to book high tea at Harrods:

  1. Go to the Harrods website and navigate to the Georgian Restaurant page.
  2. Click on the 'book now' button.
  3. Select the date and time you would like to book.
  4. Enter your details and the number of people in your party.
  5. Confirm your booking and pay the deposit.
  6. Arrive at the restaurant at the designated time, dressed appropriately.
  7. Enjoy your high tea experience!

Tips

Here are a few tips to help you get the most out of your high tea experience at Harrods:

  • Book ahead of time to avoid disappointment.
  • Arrive early to take in the beautiful views of London from the restaurant.
  • Try the seasonal menu items for a unique and delicious experience.

  • Consider purchasing a gift card for a loved one to enjoy the Harrods high tea experience.

Solution

If you are looking for a luxurious and quintessentially British high tea experience, look no further than Harrods. With its stunning views, elegant ambiance, and delicious menu, it is the perfect place to indulge in a little luxury. Book ahead of time, dress appropriately, and enjoy the experience!

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about high tea at Harrods:

  • What time is high tea at Harrods? High tea is served at the Georgian Restaurant at Harrods from 12 pm to 6 pm every day.
  • How much does high tea at Harrods cost? The cost of high tea at Harrods varies depending on the day and time of your booking. Prices start at around £59 per person.
  • What is the dress code for high tea at Harrods? The dress code for high tea at Harrods is smart casual. Men are required to wear a collared shirt, and women are asked to dress appropriately.
  • Can I bring children to high tea at Harrods? Yes, children are welcome at high tea at Harrods. There is a children's menu available, and high chairs are provided.
