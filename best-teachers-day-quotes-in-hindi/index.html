Best Teachers Day Quotes In Hindi

Teachers Day is celebrated every year on 5th September to honor and appreciate the contributions of teachers in shaping the lives of their students. It is a day to thank our teachers for their guidance, support, and knowledge. As a way of expressing gratitude towards them, we have compiled some of the best Teachers Day Quotes in Hindi. These quotes will not only help you express your gratitude but also make your teachers feel appreciated and valued.

1. "गुरु ब्रम्हा गुरु विष्णु, गुरुः देवो महेश्वरा, गुरु साक्षात परब्रह्म, तस्मै श्री गुरुवे नमः।"

This quote is a famous Sanskrit shloka which means that the teacher is Brahma, the teacher is Vishnu, the teacher is Maheshwara, and the teacher is the ultimate God. This quote emphasizes the importance of a teacher in our lives and how they are equivalent to God. It is a great way to show your respect and gratitude towards your teacher.

2. "जो अपने दोस्तों के साथ हंसते-खेलते हुए पढ़ाता है, वो सचमुच सीखाता है।"

This quote means that the teacher who teaches while having fun and laughter with their students is the one who truly teaches. It emphasizes the importance of creating a fun and interactive learning environment for students. This quote is perfect for teachers who believe in making learning a fun and enjoyable experience.

3. "शिक्षक वह है जो ज्ञान के दीप से अंधकार को दूर करता है।"

This quote means that the teacher is the one who removes the darkness of ignorance with the light of knowledge. It emphasizes the role of a teacher in guiding their students towards the path of knowledge and enlightenment. This quote is perfect for teachers who believe in the power of education to change lives.

4. "अध्यापन कला से जीवन रूपी चित्र की सजावट होती है।"

This quote means that teaching is like decorating a picture of life. It emphasizes the importance of teaching in shaping the lives of students and making them better individuals. This quote is perfect for teachers who believe in the power of education to transform lives.

5. "शिक्षा वह है जो आपको एक वर्ष में अमीर नहीं बना सकती, लेकिन जो आपको एक जीवन भर अमीर बना सकती है।"

This quote means that education may not make you rich in a year, but it can make you rich for a lifetime. It emphasizes the importance of education in shaping the future of an individual and making them successful in life. This quote is perfect for teachers who believe in the power of education to transform lives.

6. "शिक्षक वह है जो अपनी जान तक दे देता है लेकिन कभी उसे नहीं माँगता।"

This quote means that a teacher is the one who can sacrifice their life for their students without expecting anything in return. It emphasizes the selfless nature of a teacher and their willingness to go above and beyond for their students. This quote is perfect for teachers who believe in giving their all for the betterment of their students.

7. "शिक्षक को समझाना नहीं पढ़ाना होता है।"

This quote means that a teacher's job is not just to explain but also to teach. It emphasizes the importance of teaching as a profession and how it requires patience, dedication, and hard work. This quote is perfect for teachers who believe in the art of teaching and its ability to change lives.

8. "शिक्षक वह है जो न सिर्फ ज्ञान देता है बल्कि जीवन के मूल्य भी सिखाता है।"

This quote means that a teacher not only imparts knowledge but also teaches the values of life. It emphasizes the importance of a teacher in shaping the character of their students and making them better individuals. This quote is perfect for teachers who believe in the holistic development of their students.

9. "शिक्षक वह है जो अपने छात्रों को सही दिशा दिखा सकता है।"

This quote means that a teacher is the one who can guide their students towards the right direction. It emphasizes the importance of a teacher in helping their students make the right choices in life. This quote is perfect for teachers who believe in the power of guidance and mentorship.

10. "शिक्षक वह है जो छात्र के स्वभाव को समझता है और उसके अनुसार पढ़ाई करवाता है।"

This quote means that a teacher is the one who understands the nature of their students and teaches them accordingly. It emphasizes the importance of personalized teaching and how it can make a significant impact on a student's learning. This quote is perfect for teachers who believe in the power of individualized teaching and learning.

Conclusion

These were some of the best Teachers Day Quotes in Hindi that you can use to express your gratitude towards your teachers. These quotes are perfect for sending messages, cards, and gifts to your teachers on this special day. Remember, a teacher's role in shaping the lives of their students is invaluable, and it is essential to appreciate and honor their contributions. Happy Teachers Day!
